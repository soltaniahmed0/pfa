About My Olive

Revolutionizing olive tree health through AI-powered disease detection

Our Mission

My Olive is an innovative final year project developed by software engineering student, aiming to help olive farmers detect diseases early using artificial intelligence. Our system analyzes images of olive leaves to identify common diseases with remarkable accuracy.

Disease Detection

Identifies common olive tree diseases like Peacock Spot, Olive Knot, and Verticillium Wilt.

AI Technology

Uses deep learning models trained on thousands of olive leaf images for accurate diagnosis.

Sustainable Farming

Helps reduce pesticide use by enabling targeted treatment of affected trees.
